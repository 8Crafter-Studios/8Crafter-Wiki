\\offset

Offsets the pos1 and pos2 locations of the selected area.

\\offset
Dflt USM Sec Lvl: WorldEdit
Dflt Required Tags: canUseChatCommands
Categories:system, world, server, worldedit
Version:1.0.0
Undo Supported:N/A
Functional:Yes
Deprecated:No

Syntax ​

\\offset [x: float] [y: float] [z: float]
